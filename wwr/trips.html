Get in Touch

The Rapids Challenge

Brace yourself for an adrenaline-pumping experience on Class IV rapids. This trip is perfect for thrill-seekers looking for a challenging adventure. With experienced guides leading the way, you'll navigate sharp turns and turbulent waters while enjoying breathtaking views of canyon walls. Suitable for participants aged 16 and above with prior rafting experience.

Family Splash Adventure

A fun and safe rafting trip for families with children aged 6 and above. This gentle journey down Class I and II rapids offers plenty of opportunities for swimming, wildlife spotting, and enjoying the serene beauty of the river. A knowledgeable guide will ensure a safe and enjoyable experience for everyone.

Sunset Serenity Float

Unwind with a peaceful evening float along the calm waters of the river. Perfect for couples or those seeking a relaxing experience, this trip includes a scenic journey through lush landscapes with the stunning colors of the setting sun as your backdrop. Complimentary snacks and beverages are included.
